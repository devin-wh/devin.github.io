## hello, world
1. create a new blog file, named "20xx-xx-xx-blogname.md"
2. fill the blog file with folling context.
```markdown
---
title:  ${title}
author: ${author}
date: 20xx-xx-xx xx:xx:xx +xxxx
categories: [${categories-name}]
tags: [${tags-name}]
math: true
mermaid: true
---
## ${head title}
hello, world ！！！
```
3. push to `_posts/` dir
